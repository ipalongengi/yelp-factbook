Our dataset comes directly from Yelp, it's a subset of Yelp's businesses, reviews, and user data. It was originally put together for the Yelp Dataset Challenge which was a chance for students to conduct research or analysis on Yelp's data and share their discoveries.

The dataset is very interesting, the amount of information that can be extracted really depicts our behavior. We can see spikes in businesses during major holidays, such as Christmas and New Years. Then there special days such as Valentine's Day, Mother's Day, and Cinco de Mayo where we can also see a huge spikes. In addition, we can use this data to map out the cities, based on the user activity.

Since the dataset contains reviews from everyone around the world, we decided to focus on the cities with the most activity. These cities are:

  1. Las Vegas
  2. Phoenix
  3. Edinburgh
  4. Stuttgart
  5. Toronto
  6. Charlotte
